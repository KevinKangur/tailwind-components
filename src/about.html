About Tito

Tito lore

I am Tito Lopez!

My story begins on the seas ruled by the Reapers. I was a fearless captain of the Niitjate Emissaar, striking fear into every ship on the horizon.

You may ask how I got my signature look.

Well, it all began one fateful night when I was betrayed by my own crew.

They wanted my most prized possession, the Hateful Bounty Spull.

They locked my in the brig, in the bottom deck. Suddenly our ship got ambushed.

Without the help of my helmsman skills we got our ship into the broadside of the enemy ship.

Our ship was quickly set ablaze.

The crew decided to abandon ship, but I was left in the brig. I burned down with my ship, and now am cursed with my green soulflame look, forever bound to the ship I once captained.
